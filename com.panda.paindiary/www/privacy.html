This application is only for testing. Signing up may allow your name and any information you enter into the application to be available for research purposes.
All data is stored in a database hosted and encrypted by Google with all personal information (email addresses, names, health numbers) being encrypted prior to going to the database (yes that's double encrypted, so that the programmers don't see your personal information).
If you give access to clinicans or reserach teams via the settings these people will be able to access some of your information including pain diary entries. You can revoke their access at any time.
